Happy wheels - one of the most interactive games and the most played online free web browser based game of all time. There is a dark and tricky physics-based puzzling arcade game is fun for some and horrifying for others.

 

How To Play

  • Use arrow keys to move.
  • Use the space key to primary action (after ejecting: Grab).
  • Use Shift/Ctrl to perform secondary actions.
  • Use Z to eject.

 

Tips and Tricks

  1. You can pause/resume/restart on the game screen.
  2. You can change character in-game.
  3. You can view/save your replay.
